WHY
KLARWIND?

KLARWIND is a global brand name of CLK Corporation. KLARWIND, a South Korean company specializing in air quality control products, was created in response to the issues of air pollution that is present in South Korea. As Korea struggles with the negative effects of heavy air pollution, air quality control products are essential in the daily lives of its citizens. KLARWIND is continuing to raise the standards for air quality control products both domestically and internationally by providing energy efficient products. CLK Corporation has been involved in the HVAC industry since 1985 and has been making air quality control products for people by providing the safest most comfortable environments both commercially, and in homes. KLARWIND products are recognized for its integration of advanced technologies paired with creative simplistic designs and ease of use. When purchasing a KLARWIND product, you can be assured that you are in good hands with a quality product designed for your comfort and safety in mind!
