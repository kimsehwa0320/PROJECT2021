      Our CEO is a president of the ‘Korean Federation of Bocchia disabled person’ and has been hosting many Paralympics as a social campaign. Also, during the 2020 global disaster Covid-19 pandemic, the company sponsored air purifiers and air conditioners for medical staffs and people in need.

      In addition, we have been supporting in various social service activities for people who has an allergic disease and respiratory patients as a part of social responsibilities. We continue to encourage people to join this social campaign to create better social network and structure.

    • TECHNOLOGIES

      Based on the excellent technology of air conditioning systems essential for state-of-the-art buildings in domestic and abroad, we developed air purifiers to protect people, pets, and children in danger of global air pollution.

      We also design every product with HEPA H13 air filters which makes a great performance to improve air quality in your place. It removes more than 99.97% of dust, fine dust, allergic bacteria and harmful gases through 5 steps.

      In addition, we are growing as a leading company based on our long-standing know-how in eco-friendly and high-efficiency air conditioning technologies that can maintain cooling and heating at high speeds of more than 218miles per hour.
